Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SESMine pricing and plans.

Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes — you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately and you'll be prorated for the remainder of your billing period. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
What happens after the 14-day free trial?
After your trial ends, you'll be prompted to select a plan. Your data and projects are preserved. If you don't subscribe, your account moves to a read-only state for 30 days before being archived — giving you plenty of time to export your work.
Is there a discount for academic or non-profit organizations?
Yes — we offer a 50% discount for verified academic institutions and registered non-profit organizations. Contact us at hello@sesmine.com with your institution details to apply.
How is the AI Cost Predictor accuracy calculated?
Our AI model is trained on 500+ real-world mining projects spanning 40+ countries and 15 commodity types. Accuracy is measured as the percentage of predictions within ±10% of actual final CAPEX. Our current model v3.2 achieves 94.2% accuracy on our validation dataset.
Can I add more users to my Professional plan?
Professional plans support up to 10 team members. If you need more, you can either upgrade to Enterprise or add additional user seats at $49/user/month. Contact our sales team for volume discounts on larger teams.
Is my data secure and where is it stored?
All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We are SOC 2 Type II certified and ISO 27001 compliant. Data is stored in AWS data centers in Sydney (primary) and Singapore (backup). Enterprise customers can opt for on-premise deployment or dedicated cloud instances.
