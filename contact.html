Frequently Asked Questions
What subscription plans are available?
SESMine offers Starter, Professional, and Enterprise tiers. All plans include core commodity intelligence. Professional and above unlock all hubs, AI tools, and export features. Enterprise includes API access and custom data feeds.
Can I request a personalised demo?
Absolutely. Select "Request a Demo" in the contact form above and one of our solutions specialists will arrange a tailored walkthrough of the platform within 24 hours.
Is there an API for enterprise integration?
Yes. Our REST API provides programmatic access to commodity price feeds, project economics data, and AI prediction endpoints. Full documentation is available upon Enterprise subscription activation.
How frequently is data updated?
Commodity spot prices update every 15 minutes during market hours. Project economics, equipment benchmarks, and ESG data are refreshed weekly. AI model weights are retrained monthly.
What data sources does SESMine use?
We aggregate data from LME, CME, SHFE, company disclosures, regulatory filings, OEM technical publications, and proprietary field surveys across 40+ countries.
Is my data secure on the platform?
All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We are SOC 2 Type II certified and comply with GDPR and Australian Privacy Act requirements. User activity is never sold to third parties.
